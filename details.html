School

Burdwan Harisava Hindu Girls’ High School was founded on January 24, 1934, and was located at R.B. Ghosh Road, ShyamSayar South, Khosbagan, Burdwan, by a distinguished barrister of the time, Sri Lalit Mohan Bhattacharyya, with the help of the four distinguished, generous, religious, and cultural icons of the period that included Sri Lakshman Chatterjee, Sri Shyamacharan Bhattacharyya, Sri Basanta Maitra and Sri Pramatha Nath Dey.



Playground

The most serious problem of the school is its small size and area, therefore, the school does not have its own playground. The students use the small garden adjacent to the Harisava Mandir as playground. Nevertheless, the students of the Harisava Hindu Girls’ High School have done fairly well in different sports such as gymnastic, tennis, badminton, and swimming at the district, state and national level and brought laurel to the school. Now-a-days, the karate training is imparting to the students of the school, and they are being trained how to protect themselves in times of danger.



Laboratories

The school has 6 well-equipped and well-furnished laboratories for the students of the higher classes to gain practical experience of the theoretical knowledge. The Science Faculty has three laboratories, one each for Physics, Chemistry and Biology. Faculties of Nutrition and Geography also have their laboratories to train students in dietary practical, and the cartographic techniques. There is a well-equipped computer section in the school, besides a music room.



Facility

There is a provision for awarding half-free studentship for a few students, but the half free ship is awarded on the strict consideration of the financial condition of the parents of the students, to be determined by the competent authority, and a certificate to this effect has to be produced, while applying for the free ship. At present, the Burdwan Harisava Hindu Girls’ School has approximately 1700 students, drawn from both, the Madhyamik and the Higher Secondary sections. It has rich infrastructural facilities that include a rich library with reading room, good laboratories of different science faculties including Nutrition and Geography and a well-furnished computer room.
